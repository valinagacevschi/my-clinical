This app is your symptoms and treatment monitor that helps you to keep an asynchronous communication with your doctor. You can complete the evaluation at your convenience and the doctor will be able to review and prioritise your results when back in office.

This will help to have your physical follow up appointment based on your clinical needs.

The app has different clinical modules with three components: symptoms assessment, medication compliance and monitoring and notifications.

Symptoms assessment

  • You will receive a code with a specific clinical module from your doctor.
  • Can be done quickly, by pressing the large symptoms button. Please answer by pressing green, yellow or red on individual questions.
  • If the app identifies a change in symptoms, you may be asked to complete a more formal questionnaire, which is usually a validated test tool for your condition.

Medication compliance & monitoring

  • Your module might have a medication compliance component.
  • You will need to confirm the administration of the treatment by checking the specific buttons.
  • You can confirm the administration of the treatment only for the last 24h.

Notifications

  • The app may send you prompts and notifications to remind you of the time to record symptoms or medications.
  • Depending on your assessment the doctor may send you additional notifications in the form of a coded message.
  • You are encouraged to act and confirm the actions required by the notifications.

WARNING

  • For any acute medical problems please proceed with engaging the medical profession as you would normally do in the absence of this app.
  • The information generated by the app is not visible immediately to your doctor, but will be reviewed at a latter time.
  • This app does not replace the telephone which you should use for calling the ambulance or calling for help in any medical emergency.
